Bio

Jun, a virtuoso pianist and accomplished accordionist, embarked on a musical journey at the tenager of 5 when the ivory keys of the piano first beckoned. The allure of music proved irresistible, and by the age of 10, Jun had added the accordion to an ever-expanding repertoire of talents. Jun's dedication to the craft quickly bore fruit, earning numerous prestigious awards throughout her musical career. The hallowed halls of the Central Conservatory of China witnessed Jun's talent, where they garnered top certificates for piano and accordion that illuminated her path toward excellence.

Continuing to ascend the musical echelons, Jun pursued a master's degree in piano performance at Wesleyan College. Her unwavering commitment to the artistry of the piano led her explore and interpret the works of classical and romantic composers with unparalleled depth and sensitivity. Yet, Jun's musical palette is not confined solely to the classics; it also encompasses the lively and syncopated rhythms of ragtime and the captivating melodies of pop music, ensuring an eclectic and diverse performance repertoire.

Beyond the realm of music, Jun's intellectual prowess extends into the scientific domain. As a dedicated scientist, she applies her analytical skills to the study of data and engineering, demonstrating a multifaceted talent that transcends the boundaries of both art and science. In Jun's harmonious world, music and science coalesce, forming a symphony of creativity and innovation that captivates audiences and unlocks the mysteries of data-driven discovery. With a profound dedication to both their musical and scientific pursuits, Jun continues to inspire and enrich her life with her unique blend of talents and boundless passion.
